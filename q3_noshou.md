| Accessible money transfers | I am a person who needs to send and receive money from people who may have low literacy or be unfamiliar with technology. I need a user-friendly application that allows me to quickly and securely transfer funds, track my transactions, and is intuitive enough that I can demonstrate its use to others.| - Users can initiate a money transfer with a single step.<br> - The app's interface relies on clear visual icons and colors, with minimal text.<br> - A demo or guided tutorial mode is available to walk new users through the app's functions. <br>- The user can view a list of past transactions with simple visual indicators.|
| Finances for innumerate individuals | I am a professional who has difficulty with numbers and needs a mobile wallet to manage my finances. I need a solution that uses visual cues and symbols to help me understand my balance and transactions without relying on numerical figures. | - All interfaces involving money/balances are based on physical depictions of the user's currency <br> - Interface does not rely on numbers  <br> -Interface is simple and minimal | 
| Economic Participation | As the central bank of an over-exploited nation with high rates of illiteracy and a large informal economy, we want a secure, mass-adoptable banking solution. This is to increase formal economic participation and provide a stable financial system for our citizens. | - Platform must be secure and meet regulatory standards<br> -Solution must be lightweight, well documented, and scalable for mass adoption<br> -Onboarding must be completed in under 30 minutes without a physical bank branch |
