| Title | Description | Acceptance Criteria |
|---|---|---|
|**[Accessible Money Transfers]** | I am a person who needs to send and receive money from people who may have low literacy or be unfamiliar with technology. I need a user-friendly application that allows me to quickly and securely transfer funds, track my transactions, and is intuitive enough that I can demonstrate its use to others.| - Users can initiate a money transfer with a single step.<br> - The app's interface relies on clear visual icons and colors, with minimal text.<br> - A demo or guided tutorial mode is available to walk new users through the app's functions. <br>- The user can view a list of past transactions with simple visual indicators.|
| **[Intuitive Interface]** | I am a professional who has difficulty with numbers and needs a mobile wallet to manage my finances. I need a solution that uses visual cues and symbols to help me understand my balance and transactions without relying on numerical figures. | - All interfaces involving money/balances are based on physical depictions of the user's currency <br> - Interface does not rely on numbers  <br> -Interface is simple and minimal | 
| **[Mass Adoption]** | As the central bank of an over-exploited nation with high rates of illiteracy and a large informal economy, we want a secure, mass-adoptable banking solution. This is to increase formal economic participation and provide a stable financial system for our citizens. | - Platform must be secure and meet regulatory standards<br> -Solution must be lightweight, well documented, and scalable for mass adoption<br> -Onboarding must be completed in under 30 minutes without a physical bank branch |
| **[Error Prevention/Proper Alerting]** | As an **[illiterate or innumerate user**], I want **[clear audio/visual alerts]** for things such as a low balance, or attempting to send more than what's in my account, so that I can **[avoid overdrafts or mistakes]**. | - [Errors are displayed with distinct icons] <br> - [Audio alerts explain the issue in simple terms.] <br> - [The errors should not scare users away or be complex] |
| **[Payment Confirmation]** | As an **[innumerate user]**, I want the app **[to guide me and confirm a payment with clear pictographs/audio]**, so that I can **[avoid mistakes and be confident when using the app]**. | - [Payment flow with confirmation is simple and easily broken down] <br> - [Upon confirmation, there should be positive validation for the end user] <br> - [If there is a mistake noticed, it should be easy and obvious on how to change it and fix it.] |
| **[Requesting Money]** | As a **low-literacy user**, I want **a simple way to request money from others using clear icons**, so that I can **easily ask for help or payments without confusion**. | - [User can create a money request with a clear “request” icon] <br> - [Requested amount is displayed using visuals such as stacked coins or bars, not just numbers] <br> - [Recipient receives a clear visual notification of the request] <br> - [Requester receives a visual confirmation once the request is accepted or declined] |
| **[Depositing Money from Bank]** | As a **low-literacy user**, I want **a simple way to deposit money from my external bank account into Orali Money**, so that I can **easily add funds without confusion or mistakes**. | - [Deposit option uses a clear “money in” icon with distinct color coding] <br> - [Guided steps with simple visuals walk the user through linking/selecting their bank] <br> - [Deposit confirmation is shown with a positive visual (e.g., growing coins)] |
| **[Withdrawing Money to Bank]** | As a **low-literacy user**, I want **a clear and simple way to withdraw money from Orali Money into my external bank account**, so that I can **securely access my funds outside the app**. | - [Withdrawal option uses a clear “money out” icon with distinct color coding] <br> - [Guided steps with simple visuals explain the process] <br> - [Transaction ends with a confirmation receipt using clear visuals] |
| **Receiving Money** | As an **adult user with low literacy and numeracy skills**, I want **clear and simple notifications when I receive money**, so that I can **immediately know and confirm the transaction without confusion**. | - After money is received, the user receives an audio and visual alert with appropriate icons. <br>
| **Checking Past Transactions** | As an **adult user with limited literacy skills**, I want to **check past money transactions using icons or voice**, so that I can **keep track of my financial activity without needing to read written records**. | - When I open the transaction history and select a past transaction, I should be able to tap an audio icon to hear a spoken summary of the transaction. 
| **Authentication - Sign In/Sign Up** | As an adult user, I would like to access their banking services securely and have a history of my transactions that I can refer back to when needed. | I should be able to sign in and have access to a history of my transactions in the app.
| **Onboarding/Demo Feature** | As an adult user who might not be extremely tech savvy, I would like there to be a demo transaction or walkthrough that familarizes me with the components of the app and how it should be used. | Upon opening the app the first time, there should be a walkthrough that shows me how to interact with the basic features of the app.
